GMTK 2025

2025-08-03 • Nicolas Gatien • flow

Last weekend I participated in GMTK 2025 with one of my friends and produced "On Repeat" in 96 hours.


The theme for this jam was "Loop" and our minds immediately went to "music loops", so we made a rhythm game about running around a vinyl disk hitting notes on beat.

Unlike many of my past game jams, I decided to take this one a little easier. It was great to collaborate with someone who had never participated in a jam before, and realizing our idea together.

My friend took charge of all the musical elements, and I made the art and code for the game.
You can try it out here: https://nicolasgatien.itch.io/on-repeat
