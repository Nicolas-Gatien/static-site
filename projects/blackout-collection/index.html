Blackout Poetry Collection

• Ongoing Updated 130 days ago

Inspired by some of the works of Austin Kleon I am working on a blackout poetry collection. This is a slow-burn art project. I make a few poems here and there, and a few years from now I will have revealed enough poems to select the best ones and publish them as a collection.
