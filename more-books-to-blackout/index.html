More Books to Blackout

2025-08-19 • Nicolas Gatien • flow

At the start of this month I finished the entirety of "Speaking of Operations" and turned each page into a blackout poem: speaking-of-operations

When I started making this kind of poetry in June and created my-first-blackout-poems, I had first gone a thrift store and bought a couple books. Now that I finished one of those two books, I allowed myself to go back to the thrift store and get two more.


I got Deadline by John Dunning because it's a completely different genre of book than my other sources at the moment.

I'm looking forward to creating poetry from these books and sharing it here.
